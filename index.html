About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate at the University of Maryland's Information School, where I work with Dr. Amanda Lazar at The Health Aging and Technology Lab. My research interests span the fields of accessibility and inclusive design, health informatics, and Human-Computer Interaction.

I am interested in studying how technology becomes incorporated into older adults’ lives, and the interaction occurs during it. Specifically, I have studied how older adults coordinate and utilize different sociotechnical resources to attain certain technology use.

Prior to my doctoral studies, I worked with Dr. Sang Won Lee at Virginia Tech. His research aims to create interactive systems that facilitate empathy among users.
